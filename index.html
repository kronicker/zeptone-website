THE BAND

Just rock!

ZepTone was formed in high school days, around 2008/09, by a couple of good friends Ivan and Antonio.Antonio at the time didn't play any instrument and Ivan was playing the guitar. Anyway Ivan saw that they share the same passion for music so he invited Antonio to come and listen to a few guitar classes. Antonio became interested and he started playing bass. The band became more active near 2012 when it got it's first drummer, and by being active I don't mean live shows, but productive song writing and making music. At the time band also had some frequent singer changes.Later Ivan saw the talent for singing in his first cousin Luka who at the time was to young (15 years old) to join the bend, but as he grew up he happily accepted the invitation. Music style is versatile, It's all about rock 'n' roll, strong riffs, heavy rhythms, but also slow and melodic parts. The sky is the limit. Songs are about everyday situations in life, and thinking processes of an individual.
